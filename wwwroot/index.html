What is UChat?

UChat is a free with no registration requirnment to talk to strangers and meet new friends. If u r a stem major nerd with on social life, don't worry! We'll pick someone else at random and let you talk one-on-one.
Chats are anonymous and uses end-to-end encription so no one else but you two knows the conversation, and you can stop a chat at any time.
Our aim is to make your chatting experience as fast, easy and best by using our random text chat, as pleasant, fun and successful as possible.
